Hi, I’m Adam Stone

I’ve built my career as a senior product manager in some of the UK’s leading technology companies, including Zoopla, Monzo, and John Lewis Money. That experience gave me a front-row seat to how digital products and strategies are designed, scaled, and optimised.

Technology has always been more than just a job for me — it’s a passion I’ve followed throughout my career.

I’m based in Chelmsford, Essex, and while I work with businesses across the UK, I’ve got a real drive to help ambitious local and independent businesses build a strong digital presence. Too often I’ve seen brilliant businesses held back by websites that don’t reflect their value, or poor online experiences that frustrate potential customers.

I work with a wide range of businesses — from tradespeople, cleaners, beauty and hair professionals, to accountants, estate agents, and other service providers. I also help local retailers establish their online presence and start selling with confidence. Whoever I’m working with, the goal is the same: creating a digital presence that not only looks good but actually works to grow the business.

That’s why I started S7ONE: to bring clear, practical digital solutions to the businesses that need them most. I can help with everything (see my services) from creating modern, mobile-friendly websites and improving search visibility, to setting up bookings, payments, and ad campaigns that deliver real results. I also provide simple reporting so you always know what’s working — and one-to-one coaching for business owners who want to build their confidence online.
